Mike Tully lives and works in New York. He works independently and in collaboration with artists, architects, institutions, and publishers on works spanning artifacts, exhibitions, publications, sound, websites, and writing.

In 2022, he graduated with an MFA from the Yale School of Art, where he was awarded the Critical Practice Research Fellowship and the Beinecke Rare Book and Manuscript Library Research Fellowship.
