I live and work between New Haven, CT and New York, NY and currently am an MFA candidate at the Yale School of Art. While this space is being updated, please go for a walk.

⎅

Current and recent projects include an exhibition, No White Walls, at the Yale School of Art with Salvador Andrade Arévalo, Zoila Andrea Coc-Chang, Amartya De, Miguel Gaydosh, and athena quispe, a digital book, A Bewildering Minute, for the Yale School of Art Photography Department, an annual publication, Retrospecta 44, with Immanuel Yang and Alvin Ashiatey for the Yale School of Architecture, ongoing research on the Community Memory Project, a screensaver with tarah douglas for Aria Dean’s lecture at the Yale School of Art, a typeface revival and reinterpretation of French Roman, a poster on Workers’ Rights with Pete Gamlen and the Philadelphia Legal Association for CUP, and a short book by Nora N. Khan entitled Seeing, Naming, Knowing for the Brooklyn Rail.
